Effects
	
	FLIGHT: +2G

	PROPHECY: +1B. If opponent has [DOOM], +1B* instead.

	SPLENDOR: Immune to Attack dice. Cancelled if wounded.
	
	INVISIBLE: +5 to natural defense. Cancelled if wounded.
	
	MANTIS STYLE: You are death and death is always waiting.
	
	INFECTION: Turns you into a zombie one part at a time.
	
	FRENZY: Replace all Defense dice with Reds. (Disable Metatools?)
	
	FRIGHTENED: Lose the highest die from your dice pool.
	Cancelled after dealing damage.

	STUNNED: You can't attack or defend with dice. Cancelled after one turn.
	
	DOOM: Something bad might happen. Place a 6 here to cancel it.

Traits:
	SPLENDOR: Same as status, but localized.
	
	INVISIBLE: Same as status, but localized.
	
	ELUSIVE: Cannot be struck with dice greater than 3.
	
	MASSIVE: Cannot be struck with dice less than 4.
	
	BREAKDOWN: Replaced with a different BP when maimed.
